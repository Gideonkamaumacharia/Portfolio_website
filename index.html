My Projects

Project 1

RecruitConnect

Recruit Connect Platform is a job application platform designed to bridge the gap between jobseekers and employers. It allows jobseekers to update their profile,explore job opportunities, apply for positions, and track their application progress. Administrators can post new job listings, manage applications, and update application progress. The platform features secure authentication, role-based access, and a streamlined user experience for both job seekers and recruiters. Built using React, Flask, and PostgreSQL, it ensures efficient job application management with a clean and intuitive UI.
